Biryani

Description:

Biryani is a flavourful dish, with moderate masalas and heavy flavour of the meat and other ingredients. This is made predominantly in the indian subcontinent. This is a one pot dish made with rice, a protein of your choice and vegetables.

The dish can take upto 60 mins of cooking time along with the chopping and prepping included.

Ingredients:

(For serving 2 people)

  • Rice (Basmati) - 2 cup
  • Red Onions (sliced) - 150gm
  • Tomatoes - 100gm
  • Ginger - 25 gm
  • Garlic - 25 gm
  • Coriander (chopped) - 1 cup
  • Mint(chopped) - 1 cup
  • Chicken - 300 gm
  • chilli - 3 whole
  • curd - 1/2 cup
  • Turmeric powder - 2teaspoon
  • Chilli powder - 21/2teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 21/2teaspoon
  • Garam masala - 2 teaspoon

Directions:

  1. Take 2 cups of rice, wash it thoroughly to remove the starch and soak in water till you cook other ingredients.
  2. Clean and marinate the chicken with 1 teaspoon each of turmeric, chilli and coriander powder. Along with that add half a cup of curd and 1 teaspoon of garam masala and keep it aside.
  3. Take a cooking pan and add 11/2 teaspoon of oil and 1 teaspoon of ghee/butter. Once the oil is hot, add the sliced onions and let it cook till it is golden brown.
  4. slice the chillies in half and add it in the pot. Add some salt for the onions to cook a but better.Grind the garlic and ginder in a mortar into a fine paste and add it to the pot once the onions are brown and bit mushy.
  5. Cook the paste for 3-4mins. Once the garlic fragrance is gone, add the remaining spice powders and splash some water to avoid burning of the spices and cook it for 5 mins in medium flame.
  6. Add the marinated chicken pieces and let that cook in same heat for 6- 8 mins. Close the vessel for this step.
  7. Once the chicken has released the water, the pot must be having a moderately thick consistent soup with chicken
  8. Add curd now and mix it gently. Also add chopped mint and coriander after the curd. let the stock now cook for a couple of minutes.
  9. Add 2 cups of water and let it boil. Add 11/2 teaspoon of salt.
  10. Once the water boils, drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot and cover it and cook in low flame.
  11. After 15 mins, turn the heat off and let the rice cook in steam for another 5-8 mins.
  12. Take the pot and gently mix the rice and serve it hot.
